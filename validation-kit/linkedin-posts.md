## LinkedIn Best Practices

### Post Optimization:

**Length:**
- Ideal: 1,200-1,500 characters
- Maximum: 3,000 characters (before "see more")
- Hook: First 2 lines (140 characters) - should be scroll-stopping

**Formatting:**
- Use line breaks for readability
- Bullet points with emojis (‚Üí, ‚úÖ, ‚ùå, üìä)
- Bold **key phrases** (though LinkedIn doesn't support markdown, use caps sparingly)

**Hashtags:**
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Mix popular (#LeadGeneration) with niche (#B2BAgency)
- Place at the end, not throughout

### Engagement Strategy:

**First Hour Critical:**
- Respond to every comment within 60 minutes
- Ask follow-up questions to commenters
- Tag relevant people (sparingly)
- Share to relevant LinkedIn Groups (if allowed)

**Content Types That Perform:**
1. Personal stories with data
2. Contrarian opinions (backed by evidence)
3. Case studies with numbers
4. Industry research/insights
5. Behind-the-scenes / failures

**Call-to-Action:**
- Direct (Comment, DM, etc.)
- Low-friction (not "buy now")
- Value-exchange ("I'll send you X")

### Visual Content:

**Images:**
- 1200√ó627px (1.91:1 ratio)
- Eye-catching but professional
- Charts/graphs perform well
- Screenshots with annotations

**Documents:**
- PDF carousels get high engagement
- Max 10 slides
- Design tip: Make slide 1 clickable (teaser)

**Video:**
- Native upload (not YouTube links)
- 1-3 minutes
- Captions required (90% watch muted)
- Square format (1:1) works best

### Posting Schedule:

**Best Times (B2B):**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am EST
- Tuesday-Thursday, 12-1pm EST
- Wednesday, 5-6pm EST

**Avoid:**
- Monday mornings (inbox overload)
- Fridays after 2pm
- Weekends (unless targeting specific audience)

### What Works on LinkedIn:

‚úÖ Data-driven insights
‚úÖ Authentic vulnerability

‚úÖ Asking for advice/feedback
‚úÖ Sharing lessons learned

### What Doesn't Work:
